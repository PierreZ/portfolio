404 · key not found

The coordinator asked all three replicas for this key. They all came back empty-handed. Either it was never committed, or it was tombstoned and compacted into the void.

op: read replicas asked: 3 acked: 0 verdict: NoSuchKey
cat ./postmortem.md

Leading hypotheses, in rough order of operational embarrassment:

  • The write is still sitting in someone's outbox. Eventual consistency means eventually — not now.
  • A network partition swallowed it. It may come back after the next leader election. It may not.
  • It was tombstoned, and the compaction already ran. There is no undo for entropy.
  • You're reading at a timestamp from the future. Retry with a snapshot the cluster has actually seen.

Recommended recovery — fall back to a node you trust:
