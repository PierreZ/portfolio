About Me

Hi! My name is Pierre Zemb, and I'm a French student in a graduate school of engineering science called ISEN where I'm currently studying Software Engineering.

I first became interested in software development during my academic training, and I have never stop ever since. Thanks to it, I have good computer skills. I really enjoy learning new languages or frameworks by myself, and using them to develop either Web or native apps on my free time. I'm really enjoying coding in C,C++, Java or Go and ship them with Docker, but I always end up developping Web apps.

I consider myself as a plumber developer, the one that like getting his hands dirty with distributed/virtualized/automated systems. I strongly believe in Free/Libre Open Source Software, and not because I had lunch with Richard Stallman (I'm still living in sin according to the Church of Emacs, because I'm using vim).

Feel free to have a look on my slides from my talks, my resume (la version française se trouve ici), my French blog and you can contact me by email (here's my PGP key if you have security concerns).
